Biography

farfetch’d is a melodic deep house, disco frisco bathing duo born and raised in the long-lost woods of Sweden. Finding each other one night in the northern auroran light the spirits of music became aligned. Now their quest for illuminating electronic comfortmusic has started. Wherever you may find yourself, on a mediterranean beach, in a moonlit groove at 5 AM or in the murderous plains of officedesks, farfetch'd will be there
